About AdareRent

Founded in 2024, AdareRent is the leading digital real estate platform in Hawassa city, connecting property owners with potential tenants and buyers through an innovative online marketplace.

Our mission is to revolutionize the property rental and purchase process by providing a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform that serves the unique needs of the community.

With a team of passionate software engineers in collaboration with Sidama Regional State Science and Technology Institute, we've built a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with local market understanding.
